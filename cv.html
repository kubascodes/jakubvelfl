About me

After graduating from the University of Bristol, I packed my backpack to spend the next year (2016-17) travelling and make my dream come true. I enjoy doing and learning about new things as a means of self-development. I have passion for travelling, being outdoors, especially mountain biking and running. My hobbies also include occasional cooking and programming. I am a social person always open to a good pint of czech beer.

What initially started as a 140h internship grew into a 9-month long contract. I had full responsibility over the company's main asset - their website and also worked on some smaller projects. It was really great, I met amazing people with whom I remained in touch and experienced wonderful ending to my Icelandic trip when I got to listen to some of my most favourite bands (GusGus). Bike Mechanic at SP Kolo (2015)

I spent the 2015 summer working as a mechanic in a Specialized Concept Store in Prague - SP Kolo. I acquired essential skills in bike servicing and care as well as had the opportunity to help customers in store.

Two 1-month long internships at Ceska Sporitelna (2014, 2010)

I took two internships at the Ceska Sporitelna bank. One as an intern in the Corporate Finance department and the other in the Strategic and Economic Research. Those internships were a great opportunity of getting insights of how banks operate. I believe that this experience was a brilliant complement to my studies because I was able to compare and contrast theory taught at University with how things are carried out in practice.
