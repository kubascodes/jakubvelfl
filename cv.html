About me

After graduating from the University of Bristol, I packed my backpack to spend the next year (2016-17) travelling and make my dream come true. I'm a lifelong optimist and take every day as an opportunity to try something new, do something exciting. Life is short, our most valuable possession is time! I have a strong passion for travelling and absolutely love being outdoors... You'll probably find me mountain biking or running in the hills, wild camping in the woods or doing some remote trekking. When I'm at home (not often), my hobbies also include occasional cooking, coding the web or anything technical (fixing the bike, car or building something like my speakers). I am a social person always open to a good pint of czech beer.
