SKILLS

I am a multi-talented person with a cross-disciplinary background. Instead of specialising in one field, I like variety at work.

I studied languages, mathematics, business and later informatics. On top of that, I self-learned programming in a dream of becoming a web developer. Similarly, I learned mechanics and electrics to learn how to repair my bikes and to get that old bus back on the road. As I am deeply concerned by climate change, I continue to broaden my knowledge of topics around nature - sustainability - alternative socioeconomic systems. Last but not least, I nurture my creative side with strong interests in art - design - architecture. Learning is a continuous journey.

I am the geek behind the computer screen, field researcher, seasonal harvester of fruits, brewer of refreshments, mechanic... But these are labels that don't tell the full story of who I am. I'm also a mountain biker, I'm a climber although I fear heights, I keep my balance on the slackline, I'm a traveller, I'm a surfer patiently waiting for the wave and I love playing the piano. I like working with my hands, I like to exercise my brain and stretch my body beyond the comfort zone, I like to try out new things.

While distinct and seemingly unrelated, each of these "I am" and "I like to" experience contributes tremendously towards personal development and a balanced skillset overall.
